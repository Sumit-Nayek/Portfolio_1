§ 01 About
FIG.01 — S. NAYEKKOLKATA · IN

A research scientist who treats every dataset as a question — and every model as an argument that has to hold up under scrutiny.

I work at NITTTR Kolkata on ICMR-funded projects at the intersection of statistics, machine learning, and healthcare AI. My work spans COVID-19 diagnostic systems, LLM-powered clinical recommendation tools, and skin-disease classification with deep learning.

With an M.Sc. in Statistics from Aliah University and hands-on depth in Python, R, and modern ML frameworks, I turn messy, real-world clinical data into models and visualizations that create measurable impact.
